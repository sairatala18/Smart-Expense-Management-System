Security & Recovery

How the system protects data and handles forgotten passwords.

Encrypted OTP
  • OTP Logic: 6-digit codes sent via 241fd01114@gmail.com.
  • Identity: Reset emails include the user's EM-Code.
  • Session: Codes expire once the password is successfully updated.
💡

Quick Troubleshooting for New Managers

If an employee cannot log in after approval, ensure they are using their Full Name or Email exactly as registered. For password issues, the OTP is sent to the address in the 6th column of your database.
